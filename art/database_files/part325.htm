Further Reading‌

[Gray and Reuter (1993)] provides detailed textbook coverage of transaction- processing concepts, techniques, and implementation details, including concurrency control and recovery issues. [Bernstein and Newcomer (2009)] provides textbook cov- erage of various aspects of transaction processing.

The concept of serializability was formalized by [Eswaran et al. (1976)] in connec- tion with work on concurrency control for System R.

References covering speciﬁc aspects of transaction processing, such as concur- rency control and recovery, are cited in Chapter 18 and Chapter 19.
