Tools

Most database vendors, including IBM, Microsoft, and Oracle, provide OLAP tools as part of their database systems, or as add-on applications. Tools may be integrated with a larger “business intelligence” product such as IBM Cognos. Many companies also provide analysis tools for speciﬁc applications, such as customer relationship manage- ment (e.g., Oracle Siebel CRM).
