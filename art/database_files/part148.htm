Further Reading

The E-R data model was introduced by [Chen (1976)]. The Integration Deﬁnition for Information Modeling (IDEF1X) standard [NIST (1993)] released by the United States National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) deﬁned standards for E-R diagrams. However,a variety of E-R notations are in use today.

[Thalheim (2000)] provides a detailed textbook coverage of research in E-R mod- eling.

As of 2018, the current UML version was 2.5, which was released in June 2015. See

www.uml.org for more information on UML standards and tools.
