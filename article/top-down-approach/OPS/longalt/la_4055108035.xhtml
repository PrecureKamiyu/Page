In this illustration we see Alice on the left looking rightward. Below her is her computer and a router in her network. This router connects to a router outside of her network, which connects to a second and then a third and a fourth. The second router also connects to a server labeled "Anonymizing Proxy," and the last router connects to a server as well. A blue line labeled "SSL" begins in Alice's computer and extends through her router and into the other network, through the first two routers and into the Anonymizing Proxy. A second arrow extends from the Anonymizing Proxy through the remaining routers, where it is now labeled "Cleartext," and into the final server.
