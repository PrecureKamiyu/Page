Summary


As you can see, there are lots of ways to abuse memory. Because of fre- quent errors with memory, a whole ecosphere of tools have developed to help find such problems in your code. Check out both purify [HJ92] and valgrind [SN05]; both are excellent at helping you locate the source of your memory-related problems. Once you become accustomed to using these powerful tools, you will wonder how you survived without them.
